History of American Football

American football first started in college sports. The first ever game was played between Rutgers university and Princeton university in 1869.It wasn't until 1920 until the American professional football Association was formed. Which was called the National Football League two years later. The NFL has become a multimillion Dollar business industry in less than a century.

American football is an 11 on 11 support in objective is the score the ball and rack up more points than the opposition.
